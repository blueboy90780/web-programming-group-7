Frequently Asked Questions

For Store Owners

How do I set up my store on the online mall?

Setting up your store is easy. Simply sign up for an account, fill in your store details, upload your products, and you're ready to start selling.

What are the fees for listing products on the platform?

Please visit our fees page located in the top navigation bar for more information

Can I offer discounts or promotions?

As of this moment, this feature is not available yet and shop owners are unable to offer discounts or promotions

How do I handle shipping and delivery?

At the moment, there's no feature to handle shipping and delivery for the webpage. Please refer to a third party tool for this

What if I need help or have a problem?

Our support team is available 24/7. You can contact us through email, or phone.

Can I customize my store layout and design?

At the moment, you can only customize the products that are being offered. You can configure the settings for each product such as the names, price and categories

For Shoppers

How do I create an account?

Click on the "My Account" button at the top navigation bar of the homepage and register for an account

How do I find products?

At the moment, there's no search bar feature, so browsing of products should be done by manually navigating through the different type of store and it's offerings

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and other regional payment methods. You can view the full list at checkout.

How do I track my order?

At the moment, order tracking is not a feature of the webpage yet and we have no plan to add it soon
